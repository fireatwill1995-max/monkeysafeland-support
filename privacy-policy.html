Privacy Policy

Monkey Safe Land — Effective date: 2026-03-31

1. Who We Are

FireWill Studios ("we", "us", "our") develops Monkey Safe Land, a virtual-reality game available on Meta Quest. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app saves the following information on the headset so your profile and settings persist between sessions:

  • Username and display name you create in-app
  • A one-way hash of your local profile password (the plaintext password is never stored)
  • Profile progression: role, level, XP, coins, inventory, and equipped cosmetic items
  • Gameplay preferences such as selected map, mode, graphics quality, sensitivity, and target frame rate
  • Saved multiplayer host address and port
  • Best times and run completion data

This data never leaves your device except as described in Section 3.

3. Data Exchanged During Multiplayer

When you join or host a multiplayer session, the game sends the minimum data required to synchronize the match between connected players:

  • Your chosen display name
  • Ready state and lobby status
  • In-match score
  • Player position and facing direction (transform data)
  • Match state events such as pickup collection and round transitions

Multiplayer connections in this build are direct peer-to-peer. There is no centralized relay server involved.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

The current build does not include any of the following:

  • Real-money purchases or payment processing
  • Advertising or ad-tracking
  • Free text chat or voice chat
  • User-uploaded images, video, or custom content
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers beyond what the Meta Quest platform provides by default
  • Analytics telemetry transmitted to external servers

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Monkey Safe Land is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

Note: Before public launch, verify your age-gate and disclosure requirements against Meta's platform policies and applicable local laws (COPPA, GDPR-K, etc.).

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All profile data is stored locally on your device. You can delete your profile and all associated data by uninstalling the app or clearing its app data through the headset settings.

8. Security

Local passwords are stored as one-way hashes. Multiplayer sessions use direct network connections between players. While we take reasonable measures to protect data during transmission, no method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
